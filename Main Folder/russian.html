Russian

About the language:

The Russian language, also known as the Russian language (русский язык, russkiy yazyk), is an Eastern Slavic language spoken primarily in Russia, Belarus, Ukraine, and Kazakhstan. It is the largest native language in Europe, with around 144 million native speakers.

The written form of the Russian language is based on the Cyrillic script, which was introduced in the 9th century by the brothers Saint Cyril and Saint Methodius. The Cyrillic alphabet consists of 33 letters and is arranged in a similar way to the Latin alphabet.

Russian is an important language in international politics, business, and culture. It is one of the six official languages of the United Nations, and is widely spoken in many countries of the former Soviet Union.

The Russian language is also considered a leading language in science and technology, with a significant presence in research, engineering, and technology fields.
